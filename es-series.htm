There are two models in ES-Series: ES-1208C, ES-1208

  • Compact Design - EZ-SPARK offers a 3-in-1 design, compact and yet highly efficient, with machine frame, power supply and dielectric tank occupying only 42" x 48" of floor space.

  • Rigid Construction - Reinforced cast iron construction on the bed and column, both with extra wide frame, provides great strength for temperature and vibration stability.

  • High Accuracy - Precision ground Vee and flat table ways are lined with Turcite-B to withstand long wear under heavy load. The Z axis rides on Swiss Schneeberger linear bearing ways. A sensitive DC servo motor and a precision leadscrew provide both manual and automatic control of the precision ram movement.

  • Exceptional Performance - EZ-SPARK utilizes state-of-the-art circuitry for the power supply. All electronics are transistorized, using MOS FET for enhanced performance. Dual voltage current being synchronously discharged allows fast metal removal rate and uniform surface finish. "No wear" operations with both copper and graphite are strong features of EZ-SPARK EDM. A special micro finish circuit for very fine surface finish capabilities is another standard feature.

 

  • Great Reliability - Low temperature electronics are separated from high heat components for better heat ventilation. An independent head elimination system is also provided to ensure reliable performance of the power supply.

  • Dielectric System - The dielectric tank is integrated into the base of the machine. Two paper filters are used for filtration. Flushing includes synchronized pulse flush. Safety features also include fluid level detection and optical flame sensor. A Halon type fire extinguisher is an optional accessory.

  • Easy Maintenance - All PC boards are sectioned in modular box design. Terminal blocks are employed for wire connections between modules. LED lights are used to indicate the status of the fuses and modules. Maintenance cost on the power supply is thus reduced to minimum.

  • Customer Service - EZ-SPARK  is committed to providing customer service, including on-site operator training, maintenance service and application evaluation. Our engineering staff is available to design fixtures for your special EDM applications.
