There are three categories of S-Series:

  • Power Band Saws:  S-12A, S-12H
  • Fully Automatic Band Saws: S-250HA, S-300HA, S-300HB, S-400HB,  S-460HB
  • Miter Cutting Band Saws:  S-4633SA, S-6235HA
