Wheel Flange

 

  • Lathe Tools Attachment

HSS and carbide tools can be held in the attachment. The tool has to be placed in the attachment upon a segment, which is replaceable, the duty of which is to fix the tool at the center of the attachment and the desired height.
  • End Mills Attachment

For grinding end mills the releasing are acquired by turning the attachment to the required angle. Leading finger moves horizontally and the location is adjustable according to the end mills diameter collets up to 18 mm diameter.
  • Twist Drills Attachment

For grinding twist drills; grinding the radial face of the twist drill. Point angle: 118° Grinding Capacity: 3-18mm diameter.
