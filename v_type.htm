Block / Plate Band Saws: VBS-0407, VBS-0710, VBS-1013, VBS-1316

Features:

  • Movable sawhead for space saving

  • Sawhead moves on precision linear motion guide

  • Saw guide elevation is hydraulically operated

  • Control panel moves with sawhead

  • Laser lamp measures the sizes of cuts (option)

  • Hydraulic roller pack (option)

  • Hydraulic blade tension

  • Hydraulic clamping Tungsten blade guides

  • Powered cleaning brush

  • Automatic chip conveyor

  • NC parameter setting

  • AC servo feeding

  • Dialogue setting of cutting rate or cutting feed speed

  • Self-diagnostic displays on the screen

  • Automatic feed adjustment

  • Out-out-square detector

  • Blade stalling and breakage shut off

  • Additional portable control panel

  • Rapid saw head return and forward
